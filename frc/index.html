About FRC 5599 Sentinels

We are a robotics team that competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). Every year a new robot is built, however these robots do not move themselves. As a result, it is our job as programmers to provide a series of instructions to the robot to make it follow human command. You may know this process as "programming".
